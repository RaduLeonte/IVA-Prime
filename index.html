Info:

IVA Primer is a tool that can automatically generate primers used in IVA cloning.

Short guide:

  1. Click on "Import plasmid" to import either a .gb or .dna file.
  2. Right click to make insertions or select regions to delete or replace.
  3. Import a second plasmid to serve as a destination for subcloning.
  4. Primers for the modifications will appear in the sidebar.
